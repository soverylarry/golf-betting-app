📋 How Snake Draft Works

Snake Draft Order: Round 1 goes Andy → Larry, then Round 2 reverses to Larry → Andy, and continues alternating. This makes it fair since whoever picks second in odd rounds picks first in even rounds.

Draft Pattern (15 rounds): Rounds 1-14 for main roster, Round 15 for backup player

15th Player Rule: After selecting 14 main players, each team picks one backup player. This player can ONLY be used if one of your counting top-6 players withdraws after the 36-hole cut.
